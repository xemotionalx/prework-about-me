how my dream to become a web developer began...

when i was a kid i taught myself HTML and CSS so that i could have the flyest neopet pet pages & myspace profile. i never had the attention span for homework, but i could spend hours reading and editing code (my parents were obviously not so stoked about this).

in retrospect it was pretty ignorant that when i decided on my major in college i didn't even consider computer science because 'i'm not a math person'.

fast forward through the montage of my young adult years to now... i'd been working as classroom teacher for 5 years, but i'd grown out of it. i was grateful for the experience, but with no opportunities for advancement in my career path, it was time to move on.

as i considered my options, i realized i could turn my old hobby into a viable skillset and enter a profession with abundant opportunities for creativity, growth, collaboration and advancement.

so hi, here i am! ready to do this thing!
